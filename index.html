Meet Our Team

Payal Mital

Born in England, grew-up in India. Did MS and PhD in Biomedical Sciences. Completed a Postdoctoral fellowship at U Pitt. Taught at a Parochial School in Squirrel Hill for 5 years. Have been with South Fayette School District for 2 years.

Lauren Rodriguez

Lauren Rodriguez was born and raised in South Florida. She moved to Pittsburgh in 1998 and attended University of Pittsburgh. Lauren taught in a private school in Wilkinsburg for 6 years, and has been with Hampton Township School District for 12 years.

Pharlan Ives

Born and raised in Florida, Pharlan earned a BS in Child Development from FSU, MS in Education from Duquesne University, and a K-12 Principal Certificate from the University of Pitt. She taught at Quaker Valley SD, served as the K-6 Assistant Principal at Avonworth SD, and recently joined the South Fayette SD as Assistant Principal at the Elementary School.

Deshanna Wisniewski

Born and raised in Northern New Jersey. She taught Special Education for 4 years. She has taught at Falk Lab School, then found her way to Hampton for the past 4 years.
